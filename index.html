The Team

Charlie Brown

Two years after high school our mom bought us our first Mac in 1995. I attempted to write my first HTML page and failed. 23 years later I’m developing web application software well beyond mywebpage.html. After graduating from college with an engineering degree I ventured into NYC and worked for some of the top recording studios. My web dreams weren’t as strong. But I had a tremendous background and knowledge of ripping computers apart and putting them back together even better. I used that skill a long time in many different IT positions. Landing an A+ cert doing tech support around the country. I chased a passion a for music but an even a deeper passion for the web. I got more technical and attained several positions implementing big data distributed systems as a Certified SAP Business Objects Architect in several software packages. I served Fortune 500 company's enterprise applications that sprung from canned solutions technology. After a small stint in the corporate world I decided to follow my dream and become a web developer. I went to a coding bootcamp, already having coded on my own for 1 year. I did the cohort for the polish. Know that I hope to put that polish on your project. I love Skyping with my daughter, a late night run; cooking to me is therapeutic, meditating, and hanging out with my family. Grant Fraser

I have been learning programming and coding for over half a year now. I first started learning Python and was overwhelmed by the possibilities that I could achieve. I began to research more and more about software and web design. I decided to slow down and begin with mastering web design. I want to be able to see the full picture- how computers work, inside and out. I am slowly learning everything from web development to computer security and software development including mobile applications and desktop applications. I have high hopes and ambitions for the future, and am spending all of my spare time after work and on the weekends, learning more information on coding and programming. Recently I have been focused on mastering my HTML and CSS skills, especially Bootstrap. More recently I have been focused on learning Javscript and jQuery. I am continuing to learn more every day and see a lot of potential in Javascript- a language I once never even knew and now I can do things I would have never thought I would be able to do. Becoming a professional web and software developer has become my passion and goal in my life. It keeps me feeling alive and there is nothing better than exercising the mind. Once I feel comfortable with my skills I plan on diving into the MERN stack type ideology using either Postgres or Mongo DB with Express, React, and Node. The power of Javascript is certainly amazing and it all boils down to true or false- this or that- 0 or 1. Simple choices and decisions that decide the fate of the future, that we all make collectively.
